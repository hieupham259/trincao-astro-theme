I am Pham Nhat Hieu, a software engineer based in Hanoi, Vietnam. I build backend systems and cloud-ready products with a focus on reliability, delivery, and real business impact.

## Positioning

My work sits at the intersection of backend engineering and DevOps. I help move products from requirements to production by combining application development, data-driven workflows, and practical delivery discipline.

## Education

I studied at Hung Yen High School for the Gifted before earning a bachelor's degree in Computer Science from VNU University of Science in Hanoi. During that period, I also became a finalist in a robot programming contest in 2020.

## Professional Experience

Since 2021, I have been working at Maxflow Technology, contributing to supply chain platforms and internal systems used in production environments. My experience includes backend development, API implementation, data processing, and close collaboration with deployment and operational workflows.

Alongside this role, I have also worked through internships and freelance projects across backend development, data analysis, full-stack delivery, and DevOps-related implementation. That path gave me a broader understanding of how software is designed, shipped, and operated in real business settings.

## How I Work

I am strongest in environments where backend engineering overlaps with cloud delivery and platform execution. I bring a backend-first mindset, practical DevOps awareness, and a bias toward systems that are maintainable, scalable, and useful in production.

Rather than positioning myself as a narrow specialist, I focus on connecting application logic, automation, and infrastructure decisions so teams can ship with more confidence and less friction.

## Current Focus

Today, I am focused on backend engineering, cloud architecture, and platform-minded delivery. I am most effective in roles where software quality, infrastructure thinking, and product execution need to work together.
